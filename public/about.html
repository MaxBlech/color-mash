About

color-mash is a project by design student max m. schneider. grown up at the baltic sea, now living at the north sea, he was always fascinated by the colors of the sea. when he got to know the pleasures of grids in university, he wondered what it would look like if one creates a very fragmented grid - like this is 80 x 60 - and colors every little piece in a different color. result was the progressive web app "color-mash" where one can create his own piece of colored art - just by picking his favorite colors. the simplicity and the uniqueness of the created canvas is evermore fascinating - with every click on "shuffle".
